Komito Analytics at a Glance

Komito Analytics is an enhancement for the most popular analytics software. It unlocks power of digital analytics with additional insights about visitor’s behavior.

Advanced Metrics

Advanced Komito Analytics metrics.

  • Social interaction

    Tracks social networks events.

  • Form submissions

    Tracks submitted forms.

  • Media

    Tracks HTML5 video, audio and YouTube players events.

  • Call to actions

    Tracks CTA links (mailto, tel, sms, skype, etc).

  • Logged in users

    Tracks pageviews by users logged in to social networks.

  • Print

    Tracks page print actions.
